Hi! I'm Ada, or you can called me Hongyue (泓玥).

I am a first-year PhD student at the Interactive Contents Design Lab, Tohoku University, specializing in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). My research is driven by a fascination with VR/AR and Motion Capture technologies, focusing on how we can bridge the physical and digital worlds together.

Beyond my research, I am a passionate athlete with a particular addiction to bouldering. I find a deep sense of peace and respect in everything Mother Nature offers—whether I’m hiking through mountain trails or diving into the blue. To me, exploring the physical world is more rewarding than pushing the boundaries of the digital one.

I’m always open to fresh perspectives! Whether you're interested in my research, want to talk about HCI, or just fancy a coffee chat (virtual or in-person), feel free to reach out.
